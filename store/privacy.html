GeminiTuner Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Overview

GeminiTuner is a Chrome extension that displays estimated token usage and cost while you use gemini.google.com. We designed it so your data stays on your device.

Data we collect

We do not operate a backend server and do not collect personal data on our servers.

  • Usage counters (tokens, estimated cost, budget settings) are stored locally via chrome.storage.local on your browser only.
  • API key (optional): If you enter a Google AI Studio API key in the extension popup, it is saved only in chrome.storage.local on your device. It is never sent to GeminiTuner developers.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not receive your Gemini conversations, prompts, or responses.
  • We do not sell or share user data with third parties.

Permissions

  • gemini.google.com — Inject the monitoring panel and read API responses on that site to estimate token usage.
  • storage — Save settings and usage totals locally.
  • notifications — Show local budget alerts.

Third-party services

The extension runs only on Google Gemini web pages. Google's own privacy policy applies to your use of Gemini.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact the developer email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
